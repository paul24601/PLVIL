About PLV: Interactive Library

Empowering Learning through Interactive Experiences

Our Mission

To assume an active role in facilitating academic learning and research through enhanced access to information resources and delivery of relevant services.

Our Vision

An academic library system which caters to the needs of the university and its local community through the (1) provision of an extensive collection of resources, (2) delivery of exemplary services, and (3) engagement in activities that foster lifelong learning.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Library Collection

Explore thousands of books in various categories, from fiction to non-fiction, across all genres and subjects.

Augmented Reality Experience

Enhance your reading experience with AR features that bring books to life, offering dynamic multimedia content.

Community Engagement

Join a community of learners and readers, participating in discussions, workshops, and events held by the library.
