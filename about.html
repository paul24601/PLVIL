ABOUT US

History

The Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela (PLV) Library, established in 2002, embodies the essence of academic growth and community development within the university. From its modest beginnings, it has grown into a dynamic center of learning and innovation, catering to the diverse intellectual needs of its students and faculty. With a comprehensive collection of resources spanning both traditional printed materials and modern digital resources, the PLV Library serves as a beacon of knowledge, fostering a culture of inquiry, research, and lifelong learning. Beyond its role as a repository of information, the library is a vibrant hub of intellectual discourse and collaboration, reflecting PLV's commitment to academic excellence and the pursuit of truth. As PLV continues to evolve and expand its horizons, the library stands as a testament to its rich history and unwavering dedication to the pursuit of knowledge.

Mission

To assume an active role in facilitating academic learning and research through enhanced access to information resources and delivery of relevant services.

Vision

An academic library system which caters to the needs of the university and its local community through the (1) provision of an extensive collection of resources. (2) delivery of exemplary services to our patrons, and (3) engagement in activities that foster lifelong learning.
